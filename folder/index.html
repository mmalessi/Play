About Us

Design Thinking

A way of thinking. That’s all it is. We take common sense and formulate it—making problems understandable and solution actionable. The key to design thinking is research and iteration. We start with the assumption that we are fallible and no solution is perfect. In order to reduce our uncertainty as much as possible, we apply rigorous research methods to understand the users of the problem we are trying to solve. With that research we methodically test solutions till we find something that kind of works. Then we keep going! It’s important to remember that no problem is ever solved. The world and people are complex. We are trying to strike a balance between simplicity and effectiveness.


User Centered Design

We never assume what users want. We ask them!


Human Computer Interaction Design

A rigorous discipline in academia, we try to hire design with experience in HCI. This field is interdisciplinary—combining disciplines such as engineering, cognitive science, and psychology. It’s all about understanding the complex interaction between humans and computers.


Computer-Aided Design

A class of software that helps designers create precise product models, with all relevant components, this is a favorite amongst our product designers and engineers.


Adaptive v. Responsive Design

In all cases where a client’s project requires a mobile or web product, we design for both. A website that can’t be easily navigated on a phone, is design left in the dark ages. If a website is responsive the code is structure such that it will automatically scale itself based on the size of the viewing window. If a site is adaptive that means that we’ve created pages specifically designed for a tablet, phone, or computer. Whatever device you’re on, we’ll display the specific page we designed for it.
