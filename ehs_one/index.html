Proactive Health and Safety Stewards

We support teaching, learning, and research on campus by making sure all those activities are safe for humans, animals, and the planet.


Expert

Are you a lab manager that knows all the ins-and-outs of lab safety? Let us reduce your cognitive load. We’ll come and set up your personal profile with all the materials you could ever need—making it easy to share with the newbies.

Intermediate

Maybe you’re a new PI, used to research, but not used to earthquakes. We can swing by and analyze your lab making sure you, your research, your staff, and the environment are safe in case of an emergency.

Beginner

Or are you brand new? An undergraduate research-aid trying to figure out left-from-right? We’ve got plenty of materials to help you get your footing.
