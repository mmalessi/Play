Project Roadmap

I’ve put together a roadmap of phases based on the Key Tasks section of the project prompt. Here’s a little bit about how it’s organized.



  • Overview Each phase has multiple tasks related to it. I’ve also estimated the rough amount of time it will take to complete each phase.
  • Dates There are no actual dates associated with the timeline. Once I start working with Cowen team we can adapt the timeline to the calendar.
  • Flexibility As you know, no amount of planning can account for everything. Flexibility is crucial for creative projects—as we move through the phases, things are bound to fluctuate.
  • Overlap Many tasks and some phases can be completed at the same time—e.g., phase I and II can overlap. Meeting with the team might take two weeks because of scheduling, but in the meantime I can also set up user interviews.
  • Timing I’ve buffered each phase with extra time. E.g., phase one may only take 10 hours, but I’ve allotted 2 weeks to make sure we have plenty of time to schedule in those 10 hours.

Team Roles

Who will I need on the team?

Leader

Who: That's Me!

Ownership & Charter: I will own most of the tasks of each phase. Lead the vision of the project. Interface and work closely with the Cowen team.


Designer

Who: Juliana Diaz. I work with her on design and data visualization work at Catalyst-Ed.

Ownership & Charter: She will provide feedback on design work and create some of the design collateral. E.g., Juliana is excellent and quick with making animations. I’ll illustrate the graphics and then pass them on to her to animate. She will also assist me on some of the overflow tasks.

Timing: I will need to bring her on board starting in phase II, possible phase I.

Example Tasks:

  • designing icons for a data visualization legend
  • writing code for a data visualization
  • designing menu functionality in the UI

Coder

Who: TBD. I am looking to hire a New Orlean native who is queer/trans/PoC. (Diversity on my team is very important).

Ownership & Charter: This person will work closely with me to build out the website. Mainly owning the technical areas that I have less experience in: loading speed and overall performance.

Timing: I will need to bring them on board phase VI.

Example Tasks:

  • Reviewing the functions I’ve written for the data visualizations to make sure they are written in the most efficient matter
  • optimizing animation speed by either (maybe writing the animations into the javascript)
  • choosing the best base libraries for loading speed

I have the experience to complete this entire project on my own; however, to produce award winning work, in this time frame, bringing on another designer and coder will be invaluable.
